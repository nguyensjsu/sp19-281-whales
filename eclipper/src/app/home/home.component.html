Clipper is the all-in-one transit card for the Bay Area. Click here for a current list of the systems that accept Clipper.

“Your Clipper card can hold transit passes, cash value or any combination. Cash value works on all participating transit systems. Passes and tickets are specific to each system.”

You can add value to your card as you go, or for added convenience, you can set up Autoload, which automatically reloads your card whenever your pass expires or your cash value balance falls below $10.

“Clipper offers discount cards for youth, seniors and people with disabilities.You can even use your Clipper card as an access key to bike share by linking your card to a Ford GoBike membership

“A new adult Clipper card costs $3. Clipper will waive the fee if you order your card online and sign up for Autoload. Learn how to get a Clipper card here or order one today!
